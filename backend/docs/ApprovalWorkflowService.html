Class: ApprovalWorkflowService

ApprovalWorkflowService()

Enhanced Approval Workflow Service

Handles multi-stage approval routing based on expense amount, payment method, and category. Supports auto-approval, escalation handling, delegation, and comprehensive audit trails.
